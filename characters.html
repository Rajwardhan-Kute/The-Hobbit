The Characters of The Hobbit

Bilbo Baggins

Race: Hobbit

Description: Bilbo is the protagonist of The Hobbit, a reluctant adventurer who proves himself brave and resourceful during the journey with Thorin's company.

Role: Burglar for Thorin's company, finder of the One Ring.

Gandalf

Race: Maia (Wizard)

Description: Gandalf is a wise and powerful wizard who guides Thorin's company through many perils, using his magic and wisdom to overcome challenges.

Role: Guide and protector of Thorin's company.

Thorin Oakenshield

Race: Dwarf

Description: Thorin is the leader of the company and heir to the throne under the Lonely Mountain. He is determined to reclaim his homeland and treasure from Smaug.

Role: Leader of Thorin's company.

Smaug

Race: Dragon

Description: Smaug is the fearsome dragon who has taken over the Lonely Mountain and guards its vast treasure hoard.

Role: Antagonist of the story.

Bard the Bowman

Race: Human

Description: Bard is a brave archer from Lake-town who plays a critical role in slaying Smaug and defending his people.

Role: Hero of Lake-town.
